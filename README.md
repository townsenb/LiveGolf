# How to get LiveGolf on your Android Cellular Telephone

### What you'll need:
* An Android phone running Android 7.0 or later
* [Android Studio](https://developer.android.com/studio) downloaded and Installed
* A USB -> USB-C cable (assuming that's what your phone uses)

1. From the Android studio startup screen, or under `File->New->`, select `import project from version control`
2. Choose **git**
3. Copy and paste the repo URL below into the appropriate box, and click **clone**
4. Plug in your USB cable into your computer, and the cable into your phone
5. Hopefully android studio can detect your phone, if not, look up how to fix it that ain't my problem.
6. click the green triangle 
7. golf
